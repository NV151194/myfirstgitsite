Maca

It is a Peruvian root that may balance hormones. It has a fruity taste and can be added to raw desserts, smoothies and hot drinks.

Medjool dates

They are high in fiber and a natural sweetener, which makes them useful to add to smoothies and raw desserts instead of using sugar or a syrup. I use fresh medjool dates in recipes because they are large and soft, but you can use dried dates, although sometimes you may need to soak them first
